Butter Chicken üçõ

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken
  • 100g butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, and salt for 1 hour.
  2. Cook chicken until lightly browned.
  3. Melt butter, add tomato puree, and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Add cream and spices, simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add chicken to the sauce, cook for another 10 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
