Ingredients

  • 1 cup ripe mango chunks
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Ice cubes as needed

Instructions

  1. Place mango, yogurt, milk, honey, and ice cubes in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve chilled.
