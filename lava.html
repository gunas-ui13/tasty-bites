Ingredients

  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 100g butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g sugar
  • 50g flour
  • Butter and cocoa powder for molds

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 220Â°C (425Â°F). Butter and dust molds with cocoa powder.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together.
  3. Whisk eggs and sugar until thick.
  4. Fold chocolate mixture into eggs.
  5. Add flour and mix gently.
  6. Pour batter into molds and bake for 12 minutes.
  7. Invert cakes onto plates and serve immediately.
