Spaghetti Carbonara üçù

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry the pancetta with garlic in olive oil until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl, then add Parmesan cheese and pepper.
  4. Drain spaghetti and immediately mix with pancetta and egg mixture off the heat.
  5. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.
