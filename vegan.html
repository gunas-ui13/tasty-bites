Ingredients

  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Warm the tortillas.
  2. Mix black beans, corn, cumin, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Fill each tortilla with bean mixture, lettuce, avocado, and salsa.
  4. Serve with lime wedges.
